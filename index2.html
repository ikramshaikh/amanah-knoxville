About Amanah Knoxville

Amanah Knoxville is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting authentic Islamic knowledge, scholarship, and community benefit.

Through collective effort, we aim to preserve and strengthen access to beneficial knowledge for our families and community.

About the Instructor

Shaykh Abdul Hakim Dickenson

Shaykh Abdul Hakim is a graduate of the Institute of Islamic Education (Elgin, IL), where he completed the ‘Alim program and received Ijaaza to teach. He has served communities in Pittsburgh and Knoxville through khutbahs, teaching, and mentorship.

He currently leads weekly classes and delivers Friday sermons in Knoxville, focusing on making authentic Islamic knowledge accessible and practical.
